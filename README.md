# 🎬 Netflix Content Analysis – SQL Project

## 📌 Overview
Analyzed a dataset of **8,800+ Netflix titles** using MySQL to uncover content trends, regional distribution, ratings patterns, and release growth over the years.

## 📂 Dataset
| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Source | Kaggle – Netflix Movies and TV Shows |
| Size | 8,807 rows |
| Columns | Title, Type, Director, Cast, Country, Date Added, Release Year, Rating, Duration, Genre |

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand the split between Movies and TV Shows
- Identify top content-producing countries
- Analyze content growth trends over the years
- Explore most common ratings and genres
- Find directors with the most titles on the platform

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **MySQL** – querying and data analysis
- **MySQL Workbench** – database management and query execution

## 💡 Key Insights
- Netflix has significantly more **Movies** than TV Shows
- **USA, India and UK** are the top 3 content-producing countries
- Content additions **peaked between 2018–2020**
- **TV-MA** is the most common rating on the platform
- A large portion of content has **no listed director**

## 📋 Queries Covered
1. Movies vs TV Shows count
2. Top 10 countries by content volume
3. Year-wise content growth trend
4. Most common content ratings
5. Top 10 directors by title count
6. Average movie duration
7. Top genres by frequency
8. Content added after 2019
9. Countries with most TV Shows
10. Missing director analysis
