# netflix-sql-analysis
Analyzed a dataset of 8,800+ Netflix titles using MySQL to uncover content trends, regional distribution, and viewing patterns.
Dataset

Source: Kaggle – Netflix Movies and TV Shows
Size: 8,807 rows
Fields: Title, Type, Director, Cast, Country, Date Added, Release Year, Rating, Duration, Genre

Objectives

Understand the split between Movies and TV Shows on Netflix
Identify top content-producing countries
Analyze content growth trends over the years
Explore most common ratings and genres
Find directors with the most content on the platform

Tools Used

MySQL – querying and analysis
MySQL Workbench – database management and query execution

Key Insights

Netflix has significantly more Movies than TV Shows
The United States, India and the UK are the top 3 content producing countries
Content additions peaked between 2018–2020
TV-MA is the most common rating on the platform
A large portion of content has no listed director

Queries Covered

Movies vs TV Shows count
Top 10 countries by content volume
Year-wise content growth
Most common content ratings
Top 10 directors by title count
Average movie duration
Top genres
Content added after 2019
Countries with most TV Shows
Missing director analysis
